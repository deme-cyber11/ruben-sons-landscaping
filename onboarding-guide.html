Our Growth Strategy

Growth does not happen overnight — it happens in phases. Below is the detailed 4-phase roadmap we will execute for Ruben & Sons Landscaping. Each phase builds on the previous one, establishing a foundation first and then scaling what works.

Phase 1

Launch & Foundation

Weeks 1–2
  • Deploy site to custom domain
  • Install Google Analytics 4 & configure conversion tracking
  • Set up Google Search Console & submit sitemap
  • Optimize Google Business Profile (hours, photos, categories, description)
  • Configure business email
  • Verify all 16 location pages are indexed
  • Test all forms and lead notifications
  • Set up call tracking (if applicable)
Phase 2

Visibility

Weeks 3–6
  • Begin monthly SEO optimization cycle
  • Submit to local business directories (Yelp, Angi, HomeAdvisor, BBB, Thumbtack)
  • Implement review generation strategy (post-service email/text sequence)
  • Optimize existing blog content for target keywords
  • Build local citations (NAP consistency across 20+ directories)
  • Monitor and respond to first Google Search Console data
  • Begin link-building outreach to local partners
Phase 3

Traction

Months 2–3
  • Analyze first full month of traffic data
  • Identify top-performing pages and double down
  • Publish 2–4 new blog articles targeting high-value keywords
  • Expand location page content based on search data
  • A/B test form placements and CTAs
  • Social Media Management Optional +$100/mo — platform setup, content calendar, 3–4 posts/week, community engagement, before/after project showcases
  • First monthly performance report delivered
Phase 4

Growth & Expansion

Months 4+
  • Scale what's working based on data
  • Target new keyword opportunities identified through analytics
  • Seasonal content campaigns (storm season prep, spring cleanup, winterization)
  • Quarterly strategy review and goal adjustment
  • Explore paid advertising if organic traction supports it
  • Consider Spanish-language content expansion based on market demand
  • Ongoing technical maintenance and site updates
